Wallpapers.

Apr 3rd, 2011

It’s spring, so why not get some freshness on your Desktop? ~ As of now I have 29 images in my Wallpaper folder which syncs via the amazing Dropbox. OS X shuffles my wallpaper every 30 minutes – this may seem a bit distracting but most of the times the applications I use cover all the screen real estate anyway and if I catch a glimpse, I enjoy something fresh. The rate of change with ~30 images is just allright.
